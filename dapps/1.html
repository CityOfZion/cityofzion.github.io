Competition details

The City of Zion (COZ) Council is happy to promote the first NEO dApps competition. Individual developers or teams are invited to join the NEO slack #develop channel and submit their work to compete for 10 prizes of 1350 GAS.

Registration

Registration form will open in this URL on the 16th September 2017 for entry in to the City of Zion dAPPs competition. The registration will require the slack and github username of team members together with the github repository for the smart contract sources.

Entry Submissions

Submissions must be made by 16th November 2017 11:59 EST.

Any entries after this deadline will not be accepted in to the competition.

Applications can be made in teams or by individuals, winners selection will follow the City of Zion governance item 7 voting process.

Submission requirements

Submissions must be made in the form of a minimal viable product (MVP). The dApps submitted should have been demonstrated to work on testnet environments to be eligible for rewards. Smart contract code need to be MIT licensed and with code documentation. The remainder business logic around it or supporting features can be fully proprietary.

Competition prizes

On the 26st November 2017 the City of Zion council will announce a list of 10 winners in Slack and on this competition page. Each winner will receive 1350 GAS to launch their applications, from which at least 500 GAS should be used to deploy the dApp.

Support

For the development phase the City of Zion council will provide testnet NEO and GAS for developers teams on our Slack. The winning participants are invited to join us at the #develop channel to help further their progress.

Good Luck!
